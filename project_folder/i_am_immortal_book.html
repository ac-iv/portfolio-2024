Don't Worry Dear, I Am Immortal

Don't Worry Dear, I Am Immortal is a personal exploration into the themes of mortality and legacy through the lens of font design. The project delves into the notion that while our lives are finite, creativity has the power to transcend time. By designing a font that mimics handwriting, this work aims to symbolically preserve a part of the creator's identity for future generations, extending their existence beyond the boundaries of mortality.

This book captures a journey of self-reflection, where the act of creation itself becomes a statement of defiance against the inevitable passage of time. The accompanying poem within the book serves as a meditation on life, creation, and the human desire to leave an indelible mark on the world. It is a celebration of how art and design can keep one's spirit alive long after they are gone, making the intangible tangible.
